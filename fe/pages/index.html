Our Offerings

We cater the following offerings
Player Development

Create programmes equipping college students with soft and hard skills, in preparation towards tackling a future in the sports industry. Nurturing holistic athlete development attributes such as leadership, critical thinking, identity, knowledge of self, teamwork, values, financial management and the importance of integrating sports and education.

Talent Management & Profiling

Developing Catalogue and documentation of athlete and coach profiles, creating a database of talent that can will be showcased and presented to the sports industry on a domestic and global scale. Broadening the window of opportunity for; teams, clubs, sports organizations, broadcasters, agencies, federations and national teams.

Training

Creating an on the job experience platform students to gain in-depth knowledge and firsthand experience on how all the key elements of staging showmanship and high level sporting events. From the back-end, all the way to the broadcasting and final cut showcase.

Coach Development

Enabling Professional and academic integrated development that empowers coaches to become the best, and bring out the best in their teams. Guidance and support for coaches to get up skilling, qualifications and experience that will impact the quality of the game and the caliber of talent output to be produced both from a coaching and athlete perspective.

Development

Player development, Corporate Image Coach development, Professional and academic integrated development, Talent development & management

Colleges Partners

Boston Media House

Boston Media House was created to offer students the opportunity to study and qualify for a career in media. A tech-rich learning environment together with practical experience, ensure graduates are ready for this exciting industry.

Rich Field

Richfield operates Higher Education, Technical, Vocational & Further Education Training, Distance Learning and Short Learning Programmes with campuses located across major cities and rural sectors in South Africa.

Dameline

is a private college, with 17 campuses, owned by Educor group. It was founded by Benjamin Damelin in 1943. Despite offering degrees, diplomas and other higher qualifications, Damelin is considered a college instead of a university due to the regulations for tertiary institutions in South Africa.

Ekurhuleni West Technical Vocational

Ekurhuleni West Technical, Vocational, Education and Training College (EWC) is a public institution regulated by the FET Colleges Act no 16 of 2006, as amended. ... Ekurhuleni West TVET College is situated in the Ekurhuleni Metropolitan Municipality in Gauteng Province in South Africa.

Jeppe College of Commerce and Computer Studies

Jeppe College of Commerce and Computer Studies is a Further Education & Training (FET) Institution, established to promote and uphold comprehensive education and training needs and aimed at addressing educational and training backlogs and imbalances

Central Johannesburg College (CJC)

Central Johannesburg College (CJC) is a South African college founded in September 2001, with the official declaration Technical Colleges into Further Education and Training Colleges made by the Minister of Education, Kader Asmal.

Eta College

Eta College is an international, private, single purpose college founded in 1983. It has nine campuses, and an online learning facility. The national office is located in Cape Town.

Varsity College

Varsity College is a South African private higher education provider of The Independent Institute of Education. It was founded in 1991, and is registered with the Department of Higher Education and Training.

Tshwane North TVET College

Tshwane North TVET College is situated in the Greater Tshwane Metropolis area of Gauteng. It consists of a Corporate Office situated in the inner city of Pretoria and six delivery sites (campuses) namely: Mamelodi; Pretoria; Temba; Rosslyn and Soshanguve South and North Campuses.

South West Gauteng College

South West Gauteng College is a public Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET), formerly, FET college, operating under the auspices of the Department of Higher Education and Training (DHET) in terms of the Continuing Education and Training Act, Act No. 16 of 2006, as amended.
